Project Overview

For Application 6, I worked within a simulated real-world scenario: stepping into my dream job as a software engineer at a tech company in Central Florida. The company tasked me with developing a real-time system, where timing is just as important as correctness. These systems must not only produce accurate outputs, but must also meet strict timing deadlines to avoid degraded performance, unexpected behavior, or even critical system failures.

In this scenario, I contributed to a healthcare technology project. This setting mirrors real medical device development, where tasks often require precise timing—such as delivering insulin at the correct moment or triggering an alarm within seconds of detecting an abnormal patient vital. Real-time systems like pacemakers operate under hard timing constraints, where missing a deadline could be life-threatening. This project emphasized designing reliable, responsive, and time-sensitive software consistent with the demands of professional embedded systems engineering.
